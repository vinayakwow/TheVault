What we do

The Vault is your school’s on-demand supplier — we post what’s available, you DM to order, and we bring it the next working day straight from Vinayak Superstore. No stock, no waiting — just fast, trusted delivery for snacks, imports, and accessories.

  1. Pick: See items on our Insta/WhatsApp posts or DM us directly.
